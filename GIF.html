GIF

The Graphics Interchange Format, or GIF, is a “…bitmap image format that was developed by a team at the online service provider CompuServe led by American computer scientist Steve Wilhite on June 15, 1987. It has since come into widespread usage on the World Wide Web due to its wide support and portability between many applications and operating systems” (Wikipedia).
